ABOUT OUR PRODUCT

we are dedicated to catching swearing and bad faith texts but that feels limiting. we strive to be used in a way that can be used by professionals and the average tweeter.
we went out to build something professionals could use in stressful work sessions. Imagine you need to let your coworker know you got the update, fast, but you're working on a hard problem you need to keep your attention on. A response you think is neutral to your coworker can easily sound snappy.
Maybe your a rally coordinator and you're genuinely excited about this new rally, but being out all day has drained you emotionally and everything sounds flat and unappreciative.
For Twitter specifically: we see a lot of us trying to make professional personas on there, and build friendly connections. So we thought of such a predictor could be used to try to craft a particular score range each time and keep a consistent mood with your coworkers or audience.
